My Project Problem

I have a confession to make. I've never really finished a project. I know, big secret, so here's why. I feel like many creators can relate when I say that I have a tendency to put more on my plate than I can handle. I'll have the occasional idea, become motivated, start working on it, then lose motivation and start working on something else, or procrastinate. I'll have good ideas, and I really want to share them, but I end up abandoning them after a large dose of coders-block. The thing is, right now I'm not doing this for money because I don't really need it at the moment, so it's hard to stay inspired when you're only doing this for fun. So, this year, I made a resolution. I wouldn't start a project without finishing one first, and even that's become useless, because I tend to procrastinate, and end up playing Minecraft bedwars for like a month, and completely forget about the project. But I'm happy to say that I have been working on two projects, one is a visual novel, and one is a short endless game. I really don't know when they'll be done, because I might get stuck playing VALORANT or Garfield Kart or something for a month, but I can assure you that I am keeping my resolution, and when they come out, you'll know how hard it was to finish them.

Sorry for the sadder post.

Much love (to like the two people who actually read this)
